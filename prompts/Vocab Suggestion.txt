        - 中等：There are fewer students in this class than in the last one.

      - 同義詞：“extremely”“quite”“particularly”
      - 用法：
        - Extremely: 表示程度極高，用於強調。
          - 例句：He is extremely careful with his work.
        - Quite: 表示程度高，語氣較輕。
          - 例句：She is quite satisfied with the result.
        - Particularly: 表示与众不同，也可用于强调程度。
          - 例句：This gift is particularly meaningful to me.

    - 問題：定語從句使用不當
    - 正確用法：定語從句用於修飾名詞或代詞，關係代詞需正確引導。
    - 例句：
      - 簡單：This is the book that I bought yesterday.
      - 中等：This is the book that I saw in the bookstore yesterday.
      - 複雜：This is the book that I saw in the bookstore yesterday, written by a famous author.
